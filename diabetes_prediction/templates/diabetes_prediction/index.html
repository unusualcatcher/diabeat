About DiaBeat

DiaBeat is designed to empower individuals by providing an easy-to-use diabetes prediction and health tracking tool. Here's how DiaBeat helps you take control of your health:

Diabetes Prediction: By inputting your health data, such as glucose levels, BMI, and more, DiaBeat utilizes state-of-the-art machine learning models to predict the likelihood of diabetes. This early detection system allows you to take timely preventative measures.

Health Log Tracking: Keep track of your health metrics over time using DiaBeat's logging feature. Record details like diet, physical activity, and medical test results to build a comprehensive health history that informs better decision-making.

Health Log Visualization: DiaBeat isn't just about prediction; it provides visualizations and trends from your health data. By analyzing these patterns, users can make informed decisions to enhance their lifestyle and well-being.

At DiaBeat, we’re committed to helping you stay ahead of diabetes and maintain a healthy life. Whether you’re looking to predict, monitor, or manage, DiaBeat is your partner in achieving your health goals.
